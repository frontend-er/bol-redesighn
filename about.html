Researching holistic impact

BOL in its activity aims to reach a holistic impact in the lives of people. A holistic approach to mission perceives a person as the unique, integral and inseparable creation of God, designed to live and develop among other people in the harmonious and consistent way He intends for them. Beginning of Life serves those who are deprived of such opportunities, responding appropriately to their physical needs, supporting them in the resolving of their emotional troubles, assisting them in their intellectual development and following them through their spiritual challenges. We see our task as building bridges between people, helping them avoid becoming excluded, exploited or marginalized. We are aiming to equip and empowering people despite their social status, age, gander or enthnicity to find their voice in society in order to bring hope and transformation in their families and communities.

@ Vladimir Ubeivolk

Building resilient communities

The Building Resilience in Vulnerable Communities (BRVC) programme developed in partnership with Foundation for Social Change and Inclusion UK to create alternative holistic model of education and integration aiming to transform the future prospects of children and young people together with their families and communities in Moldova as well as other countries of the region.
"Moldova is seen by many of its people as a kind of bus station, somewhere you wait for an opportunity to leave without considering all its consequences and risks. But this perspective need to be changed!"

HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE

More than 1/3 of Moldova’s population lives in poverty, and these people are at constant risk of experiencing various forms of oppression and marginalization. In response to the desperate situations faced by vulnerable groups, BOL provides humanitarian assistance, which allow needy people cover their very basic needs such as food, clothing and hygiene items.
