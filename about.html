Building resilient communities

The Building Resilience in Vulnerable Communities (BRVC) program developed in partnership with Foundation for Social Change and Inclusion UK to create alternative holistic model of education and integration aiming to transform the future prospects of children and young people together with their families and communities in Moldova as well as other countries of the region.
"Moldova is seen by many of its people as a kind of bus station, somewhere you wait for an opportunity to leave without considering all its consequences and risks. But this perspective need to be changed!"

Executive director

STUDIO OF INNOVATIVE LEADERSHIP

In seeking to fully understand deeply rooted challenges of individuals and the communities with its complex causes and effects, BOL team launched in 2014 the Studio of Innovative Leadership (SIL) - a creative training platform that involve leaders and their teams in an alternative on-going education and best practices in the local contexts. In SIL leaders are shaped and changed.
"Along with the equipping practitioners, we organize and take part in various conferences and working groups which enable dialogue between practitioners and thinkers, to enforce transformation."

President and co-founder of BOL, trainer

Dr. Vladimir Ubeivolc

SUSTAINABILITY PROJECTS
